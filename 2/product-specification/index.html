Product Specifications

The NISAR mission produces a range of data products, including Level 0 unfocused raw data, Level 1 Range-Doppler products, Level 2 Geocoded products and Level 3 Geophysical products, as described in the other content in this section. Here is a list of the product specification documents for each of the mission-generated products available for NISAR.

  • Level-0B Radar Raw Signal Data and Calibration Product Hawkins, 2025

  • Level-1 Range Doppler Single Look Complex Hawkins, 2025

  • Level-2 Geocoded Single Look Complex Fattahi, 2025

  • Level-1 Range Doppler Wrapped Interferogram Brancato et al., 2025

  • Level-1 Range Doppler Unwrapped Interferogram Brancato et al., 2025

  • Level-2 Geocoded Unwrapped Interferogram Brancato et al., 2025

  • Level-2 Geocoded Polarimetric Covariance Shiroma, 2024

  • Level-1 Range Doppler Pixel Offsets Brancato et al., 2025

  • Level-2 Geocoded Pixel Offsets Brancato et al., 2025

  • Level-3 Soil Moisture Huang & Chan, 2025
